Federal / FERC Actions

Dec 18, 2025
FERC PJM Colocation Order — Directed PJM to create 3 new transmission service tiers (Firm, Non-Firm, Interim) for DC co-location at power plants. PJM compliance: Feb 16, 2026.
Oct 23, 2025
DOE ANPR to FERC — Advanced Notice of Proposed Rulemaking with principles for all large-load interconnections nationwide. FERC must act by Apr 30, 2026.
Feb 2025
FERC Show Cause Order (PJM) — Required PJM to justify tariff clarity for co-location arrangements; triggered the Dec 18 final colocation order.
2025-2026
FERC BTMG Reform — Behind-the-meter generation studied only at actual grid injection amount (not full plant size), reducing DC study obligations where generator serves most load locally.
